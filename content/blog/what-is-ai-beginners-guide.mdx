# What Is AI? A Complete Beginner's Guide for 2026

Artificial intelligence is everywhere — in your phone, your email, your car, your doctor's office. But most explanations of AI are either too technical or too vague. Let's fix that.

## AI in Plain English

Artificial intelligence is software that can learn from data and make decisions. Instead of programming every rule by hand, you show the software thousands of examples and it figures out the patterns itself.

## Machine Learning: The Engine Behind AI

Machine learning is the most common type of AI today. The process is straightforward: you feed data into a model, the model finds patterns, and then it can make predictions on new data it hasn't seen before.

## Why You Should Care

AI isn't just for researchers at tech companies. It's becoming a core skill across every industry. Understanding how AI works — even at a basic level — gives you a massive advantage in any career.

## Types of AI You Use Every Day

- **Recommendation systems** — Netflix suggesting shows, Spotify discovering music
- **Natural language processing** — ChatGPT, email spam filters, voice assistants
- **Computer vision** — Face unlock on your phone, self-driving car sensors
- **Predictive analytics** — Weather forecasts, stock market analysis
